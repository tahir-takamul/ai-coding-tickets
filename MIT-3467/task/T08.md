### Expected post-run state

- Namespace `cicd` (already existing) gains:
  - StatefulSet `sonarqube-sonarqube` (1 replica, Ready)
  - StatefulSet `sonarqube-postgresql` (1 replica, Ready) — bundled
  - Service `sonarqube-sonarqube` (ClusterIP, 9000)
  - Service `sonarqube-postgresql` (ClusterIP, 5432)
  - PVC `data-sonarqube-sonarqube-0` (10 Gi, Bound, `managed-csi-premium`)
  - PVC `sonarqube-extensions` (1 Gi, Bound, `managed-csi-premium`)
  - PVC for postgres (Bound, `managed-csi-premium`)
  - Secret `sonarqube-credentials` with the six keys (admin-password, current-admin-password, db-password, monitoring-passcode, saml-certificate, secret.properties)
  - SA `sonarqube` annotated with `azure.workload.identity/client-id=<MI client id from T01>`

## Acceptance criteria

- `kubectl -n cicd get statefulset sonarqube-sonarqube -o jsonpath='{.status.readyReplicas}'` returns `1`.
- `kubectl -n cicd exec sonarqube-sonarqube-0 -- curl -s localhost:9000/api/system/status` returns `{"status":"UP"}` (note: at this point may still be `STARTING` for a couple of minutes after pod becomes Ready — the playbook waits up to 15 min, but Sonar's status is UP slightly later).
- `kubectl -n cicd get sa sonarqube -o jsonpath='{.metadata.annotations.azure\.workload\.identity/client-id}'` matches the MI client ID from `terraform output sonarqube_workload_identity_client_id`.
- `kubectl -n cicd get pvc | grep sonar` shows `data-sonarqube-sonarqube-0` 10 Gi Bound + `sonarqube-extensions` 1 Gi Bound + postgres PVC Bound — all `managed-csi-premium`.

## Failure modes & remedies

- **Pod CrashLoopBackOff with `STS authentication failed`** → SA annotation didn't apply; check step 10 of T06 and re-annotate manually.
- **Postgres pod stuck Pending** → PVC didn't provision; verify `managed-csi-premium` storageClass exists in the cluster (`kubectl get sc`).
- **Sonar pod stuck Pending with `vm.max_map_count` errors** → `sysctlInitContainer.enabled: true` should handle this, but if AKS node is locked down, the privileged init may be denied. Confirm with platform; may need a node-pool tweak.
- **Helm timeout (15 min not enough)** → Sonar cold start with first-time DB migration can take longer. Bump `--timeout 25m0s` in the playbook.
