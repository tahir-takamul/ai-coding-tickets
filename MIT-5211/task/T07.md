## Reason

APISIX is `ClusterIP`-only. External traffic reaches it through the
existing silmarils Cloudflare Zero Trust Tunnel (`silmarils-dev`
tunnel — name is historical; scopes the whole silmarils AKS cluster).
Exposing the QA APISIX requires one new `silmarils_ingress_rules`
entry pointing the public hostname at the in-cluster Service. No new
tunnel, no Access policy, no DNS hand-editing (the CNAME is auto-created
by the existing `cloudflare_record.silmarils_tunnel_ingress` resource).
